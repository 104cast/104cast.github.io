What is Shabbat?

Shabbat is a day of rest and celebration that begins on Friday at sunset and ends on the following evening after nightfall. The pinnacle of Shabbat is the traditonal Friday night meal, which acts as a time for the community to come together and celebrate after a long week.

Join us as the Cornell community comes together on April 28th to celebrate all our acheivements this year. This beautifull meal will feature five-star kosher cuisine, performances from some of Cornell's leading acapella groups, and a plethora of other fun activities - all with 1,000 of your closest friends! Space is limited, so please be sure to signup today!
